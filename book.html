The Anatomy of Failure

This conceptual book explores failure through a visceral lens, literally. Each spread pairs emotional experiences with anatomical organs, using the human body as a metaphorical map for the weight and complexity of failure. From the heart symbolizing vulnerability to the skin echoing the imperfections of first impressions, the project bridges physical form with emotional depth. "The Anatomy of Failure" invites readers to confront, understand, and reframe failure as an essential, embodied part of growth. The final page leaves the narrative intentionally unresolved, emphasizing that failure is not a fixed point but a continuous, reshaping, and evolving experience. Rather than offering closure, the book invites reflection framing failure as a living process rather than an end.
